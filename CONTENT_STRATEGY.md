# Content Creation Strategy

Turn the poker analytics into a YouTube channel and content funnel that drives users to the product.

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: "Are You Profitable?" (Top of Funnel)
Hook: most poker players think they're better than they are.

**Video Series:**
- "80% of poker players are losing — here's the math"
- "What your cash rate ACTUALLY tells you about your skill"
- "I analyzed 100,000 players — here's why you're stuck"
- "The 7 leaks killing your poker game (which one is yours?)"
- "Too Aggressive? The data says you're not aggressive ENOUGH — you're aggressive in the WRONG SPOTS"
- "The min-cash trap: why cashing feels good but costs you money"

**Format:** Data-driven breakdowns using our actual model outputs. Screen recordings of the analysis, charts, real numbers. 5-10 minute videos.

**CTA:** "Want to know your exact leak? Try our free assessment tool" → drives to the web app.

### Pillar 2: "Fix Your Game" (Middle of Funnel)
Teach the fixes for each leak category.

**Video Series by Leak:**

Too Aggressive:
- "GTO explained in 10 minutes for tournament players"
- "Your bluff-to-value ratio is wrong — here's the math"
- "I ran a solver on the 5 spots aggressive players spew the most"
- "Table image: how to use your aggression as a weapon"

Too Passive / Min-Cash Machine:
- "Min-cash math: why $500 cashes are costing you $5,000"
- "I changed one thing about my game and tripled my EV"
- "The 8x stack rule: what your stack at the money should look like"
- "Bubble play: when to tighten and when to attack"

Can't Close:
- "You keep making final tables and losing — here's why"
- "ICM explained: why tournament chips aren't all equal"
- "The 5 final table spots you're playing wrong (solver breakdown)"
- "Heads-up for the win: a complete guide"

Skill Ceiling:
- "The honest truth about whether you're good enough to move up"
- "Study plan: how to go from Bad Reg to Good Reg in 12 months"
- "The fundamentals that 80% of players skip"

**Format:** Solver screenshots, hand breakdowns, before/after analysis. 10-15 minute videos. More instructional.

**CTA:** "Get the full solver pack for your leak" → drives to paid product.

### Pillar 3: "Move Up" (Bottom of Funnel)
For players who are already profitable — should they move up?

**Video Series:**
- "I analyzed every tournament at the Wynn and Venetian — here's the real payout data"
- "When should you move up in stakes? (we built a model)"
- "$250 to $1,500: what actually changes at higher stakes"
- "We tracked 7 players — here's who should move up and who shouldn't"
- "Bryan vs Nicholas: same group, different results — why aggression matters at higher stakes"
- "Frankie thinks he's a pro — the data says otherwise"

**Format:** Storytelling + data. Use the real player group as case studies (with permission / anonymized). 10-20 minute videos.

**CTA:** "Run the stake move model on your own results" → drives to the web app.

### Pillar 4: "Behind the Build" (Brand Building)
Document building the product itself.

- "I'm building a poker app with AI — here's day 1"
- "How I scraped 12,000 tournaments from Hendon Mob"
- "Building a GTO solver into a bankroll tracker"
- "From idea to MVP: poker app development diary"

**Format:** Dev diary, screen recordings, casual. 5-10 minutes. Appeals to both poker players and tech audience.

## Content Calendar Framework

**Weekly Output Target:** 2-3 videos

| Day | Content Type |
|-----|-------------|
| Monday | Pillar 1 or 2 (educational, data-driven) |
| Wednesday | Pillar 2 or 3 (instructional or case study) |
| Friday | Pillar 4 (dev diary) or Pillar 1 (hook/viral) |

## Platform Strategy

**YouTube:** Primary platform. Long-form (10-20 min) for Pillars 2-3. Shorts for Pillar 1 hooks.

**TikTok / Instagram Reels:** Cut 60-second clips from each video:
- "Did you know 80% of poker players are losing?"
- "Your cash rate is lying to you"
- Quick stat reveals from the model

**Twitter/X:** Share charts, data visualizations, hot takes from the analysis. Link to full videos.

**Reddit (r/poker):** Share findings, engage in discussion. This community loves data. Don't hard-sell — let the analysis speak.

## Content Production Pipeline (using the tools we built)

1. Record raw audio (phone/mic) — your analysis and commentary
2. Whisper transcription → raw text
3. Claude cleanup → polished script
4. ElevenLabs voice clone → clean narration (optional, for data-heavy segments)
5. Screen recordings of model/solver output for visuals
6. CapCut/InVideo for final edit
7. Upload via YouTube API

## Content-to-Product Funnel

```
YouTube/TikTok/Twitter (free content)
    → "What's your leak?" (free assessment on web app)
        → Basic results + leak classification (free)
            → "Get your personalized study plan" (free trial)
                → Solver packs + full dashboard (paid subscription)
```

Every piece of content should answer a question the viewer has, then reveal that the product answers the NEXT question they'll have.
