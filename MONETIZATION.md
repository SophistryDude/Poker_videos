# Monetization Plan

## Product: Poker Development Platform
Not a bankroll tracker. Not a solver. A **player development system** that diagnoses leaks, prescribes fixes, tracks progress, and tells you when to move up.

---

## Tier Structure

### Free Tier — "Assessment"
**Goal:** Get users in, classify their leak, hook them with data they can't get elsewhere.

Includes:
- Manual result entry (unlimited)
- CSV import from Poker Bankroll Tracker / Poker Analytics
- Basic stats dashboard (CR, FT rate, profit graph)
- Leak classification (which of the 7 categories)
- Stake move recommendation (should you move up/down)
- Comparison to 100K synthetic population ("you're in the top X%")
- 5 free pre-solved spots from the solver library

**Why free:** This is the funnel. The leak classification alone is something no other tool provides. Users will share their results on social media ("I'm a Can't Close player — turns out I need to study FT strategy") which drives organic growth.

### Pro Tier — $14.99/month — "Development"
**Goal:** The core product for serious recreational players and aspiring regs.

Everything in Free, plus:
- Full solver study library (200+ pre-solved spots organized by leak)
- Personalized study plan based on their leak
- Aggression analysis and recommendations
- Field-normalized stats (input your typical field size)
- 50-tournament rolling reassessment with milestone alerts
- Progress tracking over time (is your leak improving?)
- Advanced stats: EV by position, by tournament stage, by venue
- Community features: compare stats with friends (like your group of 7)
- Export reports (PDF for coaching, sharing)

### Elite Tier — $39.99/month — "Mastery"
**Goal:** For regs, aspiring pros, and players moving up in stakes.

Everything in Pro, plus:
- Custom solver runs (input your own spots, get GTO solutions)
- ICM-adjusted FT analysis
- Node locking: input opponent tendencies, get exploitative adjustments
- Stake move model with full 4-tier projection
- Bankroll management tools with variance simulation
- Multi-table tracking (play 2-3 tournaments simultaneously)
- Priority support / coaching queue
- API access (for players who want to build on top of the data)

### Coaching Add-on — $99/session
- 1-on-1 video review using the platform's data
- "Here are the 5 spots costing you the most EV" — generated by the system, reviewed by a coach
- Could eventually be AI-coached using solver output + leak context

Nobody else closes that loop. PioSolver is a tool for experts. We're a development system for everyone.

## Growth Levers

### Organic (Free)
- YouTube content driving free assessments
- Social sharing: "I'm a [leak type] player according to [app name]" — viral loop
- Reddit r/poker engagement (data-driven posts perform well)
- Word of mouth in poker rooms ("what app is that?")

### Paid (Later)
- YouTube ads targeting "poker strategy" searches
- Poker podcast sponsorships
- Affiliate partnerships with poker rooms (they benefit from educated players staying in the game longer)
- Tournament series partnerships (Wynn, Venetian — offer the tool to their players)

### Partnership Opportunities
- **Poker rooms:** They want players to keep playing. A tool that helps players improve = longer customer lifetime. Could offer the tool for free at check-in.
- **Poker training sites:** Cross-promotion. We do assessment, they do deep coaching.
- **Poker media:** Content partnerships with Card Player, PokerNews, etc.

## MVP Priority

For launch, we need:
1. **Free tier working** — CSV import, leak classification, basic dashboard
2. **3-5 content videos** published demonstrating the analysis
3. **Pre-solved spot library** (50 spots minimum) from TexasSolver
4. **Web app on EC2** with user accounts

That's a viable MVP to start collecting users and validating demand before building the Pro/Elite tiers.
